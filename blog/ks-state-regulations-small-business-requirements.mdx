**Kansas (KS) State Requirements**
----------------------------------

\- Incorporating Your Business

\- Business Property Assessment

\- State Labor Laws

\- State Withholding Tax

\- Sales Tax

\- Special Licenses, Permits, Inspections

\- Insurance Requirements

Kansas (KS) State Requirements for Small Business can be found in **[this website.](https://ksbiz.kansas.gov/start/obtain-licenses-and-permits/)**

**Small Business Federal Requirements**
---------------------------------------

Most common Federal Requirments are:

\- Federal taxes

\- Affordable Care Act (for businesses with 50 or more employees)

\- Federal licenses, permits or certificates

\- Marketing and advertising laws

\- Copywright laws

\- Workplace poster laws

\- Workplace health and safety laws

\- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

For more details on the US Federal Regulations, please refer to **[this section.](../federal-regulations/)**
