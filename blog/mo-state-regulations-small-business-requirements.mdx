**Missouri (MO) State Requirements**
------------------------------------

\- Incorporating Your Business

\- Business Property Assessment

\- State Labor Laws

\- State Withholding Tax

\- Sales Tax

\- Special Licenses, Permits, Inspections

\- Insurance Requirements

Missouri (MO) State Requirements for Small Business can be found in **[this website.](https://www.sos.mo.gov/business/outreach/starting_steps)**

**Small Business Federal Requirements**
---------------------------------------

Most common Federal Requirments are:

\- Federal taxes

\- Affordable Care Act (for businesses with 50 or more employees)

\- Federal licenses, permits or certificates

\- Marketing and advertising laws

\- Copywright laws

\- Workplace poster laws

\- Workplace health and safety laws

\- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

For more details on the US Federal Regulations, please refer to **[this section.](../federal-regulations/)**

Checklist for Small Business Compliance
---------------------------------------

\- Zoning Regulations

\- Business License

\- Business Name Registration

\- Legal Structure

\- State Taxes

\- Property Assessment

\- State & Federal Labor Laws

\- State Withholding

\- State Unemployment Insurance

\- Workers’ Compensation Insurance

\- Permits, Licenses & Inspections

\- Employer Identification Number (EIN)

\- Federal Unemployment Tax (FUTA)

\- Health Insurance (Affordable Care Act)
