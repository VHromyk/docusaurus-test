City compliance requirements​

City regulations can affect small business operations. The local regulatory requirements that may apply to small businesses are:

1. Zoning Permits

2. City Business License

3. Building Permits

4. Site Inspection

5. Local Taxes

For More details, check local municipality website

Small Business Federal Requirements​

Most common Federal Requirments are:

- Federal taxes

- Affordable Care Act (for businesses with 50 or more employees)

- Federal licenses, permits or certificates

- Marketing and advertising laws

- Copywright laws

- Workplace poster laws

- Workplace health and safety laws

- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

For more details on the US Federal Regulations, please refer to this section.
